About PeterSyn Research and Development LLC

Petersyn Research and Development is a registered LLC under Nevada State Law. The company was founded under the realization that there existed gaps in understangings of how true natural intelligence works. We realized that if we are to move away from the traditonal methods of statistical and probablistic intelligence models, there needed to be investigations into how nature organizes thought and intelligence.


Currently, Petersyn R&D is reaching out to academic individuals and groups, institutions, and organizations dedicated to studying either intelligence itself or its constituent parts. This outreach is key to forming collaborative experiemental code projects and eventually research projects when we run up against the limits of knowledge on the subject.
It is our belief that like all other forms of science known to man, the science of intelligence and consciousness are deducible to its simplest component parts. Parts that when individually understood, can be explained as a whole macroscopic system. Once these principlese are understood, a completely ground up approach can be taken to recreate intelligence in digital form. What we and others call, Synthetic Intelligence. Moving away from the the temptation to merely imitate the results of intelligence will allow us to finally discover what intelligence is and why it is arguably one of the most powerful forces we have seen.
