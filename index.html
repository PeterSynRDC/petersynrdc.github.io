About Us

We're Dedicated

Ensuring that your company is up to par with other the big names in AI is not just a job to us. We started this company because we believed there was a imbalance that needed to be corrected. We won't quit until the job is done, even if we have run out of our intial contract money. If you have felt like you were misled at the end of the contract, don't pay the remainder. We're in this until the end. Money or not. It's a passion, not just a job.

We're Autodidacts

The 10 years of programming experience and 3 years of AI research and experience was all self taught. There was no tests telling us how well we can regurgitate code out. We learned and understand the core fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence because we had to teach ourselves from the ground up. We have a deep intuitive understanding, not just a top down "good enough" overview.

We're Honest

There is a huge problem in Artifical Intelligence with researchers lying or hiding the truth about what they know and can do. Many large non-AI companies still think of AI as some sort of magic black box systems. We aim not only to deliver our service truthfully but by the end of our contract leave you with a strong enough understanding to pave your own way.
